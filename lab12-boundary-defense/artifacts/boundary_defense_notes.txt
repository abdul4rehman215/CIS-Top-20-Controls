Lab 12 - Boundary Defense Notes
Date: 2026-02-28
System: Ubuntu 24.04.1 LTS

Firewall: UFW
- Default incoming: deny
- Default outgoing: allow
Rules:
- Allow 22/tcp (SSH)
- Deny 23 (Telnet)
- Deny 8080 (unused inbound port)

Testing:
- nc -vz 172.31.10.207 8080  -> Connection timed out (blocked)
- nc -vz 172.31.10.207 22    -> succeeded (allowed)
